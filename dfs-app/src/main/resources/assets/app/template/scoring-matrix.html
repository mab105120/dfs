Scoring Instructions

On the next page you will be asked to use a 1-100 scale to evaluate teachers' performance on three job dimensions: Student Learning, Instructional Practice, and Professionalism. To better understand how to assign scores for each dimension please review the following details:

Student Learning

Tenure applicants must provide consistent evidence showing that all students, including those with special needs, perform competitively on state standard exams for academic qualifications.

Evaluation Score Explanation
Exceeds expectation 75-100 Multiple sources of evidence showing that virtually all students, including special populations, achieve substantial gains on standard exams
Meets expectation 50-75 Multiple sources of evidence showing a majority of students, including special populations, achieve substantial gains on standard exams
Doesn't fully meet expectation 25-50 Evidence showing a majority of students, including special populations, achieve gains on standard exams.
Below expectation 1-25 Evidence that the majority of students do not achieve gains on standard exams.
Instructional Practice

Tenure applicants must provide consistent evidence indicating practice at the most effective level in the categories: • Planning and Preparation • Classroom Environment • Instruction

Evaluation Score Explanation
Exceeds expectation 75-100 Multiple sources of evidence indicating practice at the most effective level in the following categories: • Planning and Preparation • Classroom Environment • Instruction
Meets expectation 50-75 Multiple sources of evidence showing a majority of students, including special populations, achieve substantial gains on standard exams
Doesn't fully meet expectation 25-50 Evidence showing a majority of students, including special populations, achieve gains on standard exams.
Below expectation 1-25 Evidence that the majority of students do not achieve gains on standard exams.
Professionalism

Tenure applicants must provide consistent evidence of high level professionalism on activities including: • Professional Growth and Reflection • Collaboration and Engagement with the School Community • Communication with Families

Evaluation Score Explanation
Exceeds expectation 75-100 Multiple sources of evidence of professionalism at the highest level: • Professional Growth and Reflection • Collaboration and Engagement with the School Community • Communication with Families
Meets expectation 50-75 MMultiple sources of evidence of effective professional contributions: • Professional Growth and Reflection • Collaboration and Engagement with the School Community • Communication with Families
Doesn't fully meet expectation 25-50 Limited evidence of effective professional contributions, or evidence of inconsistent or inadequate professional contributions: • Professional Growth and Reflection • Collaboration and Engagement with the School Community • Communication with Families
Below expectation 1-25 Negative evidence of professional contributions: • Professional Growth and Reflection • Collaboration and Engagement with the School Community • Communication with Families

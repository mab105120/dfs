Background

Every year select high schools must decide which teachers would be promoted for tenure. This year, in an initiative to improve its tenure selection process, the tenure board at Kingston High School (K.H.S) * decided to solicit outside input on its promotion decisions.


We ask for your help!


In what follows we ask you to answer basic questions about your experience with providing performance reviews. Afterwards you will be asked to evaluate two teachers side by side. For each teacher we will provide you with a list of performance reviews provided by the teacher's supervisors on relevant job functions. After reading supervisor reviews, we ask you to recommend one of the two teachers for the tenure promotion.


Please be mindful of the impact your decisions will have on personnel selection.


* In order to protect the school's privacy and make sure any previous knowledge of the school doesn't influence your response, we are using the Kingston High School (K.H.S) pseudonym. Any resemblance to any educational facilities is purely coincidental.

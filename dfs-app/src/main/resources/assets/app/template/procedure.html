Background

Every year select high schools must decide which teachers would be promoted for tenure. This year, in an initiative to improve its tenure selection process, the tenure board at Kingston High School (K.H.S) * decided to solicit outside input on its promotion decisions.

Every year select high schools must decide which teachers would be sent to the Annual Summer Teacher Conference. The conference, spread over one week, helps under-performing teachers build skills that allow them to improve their performance and enhance their careers. Unfortunately, only limited spots are available so struggling teachers are considered in priority. This year, in an initiative to improve its selection process, the Teacher Training Committee at Kingston High School (K.H.S) * decided to solicit outside input on its training selection decisions.

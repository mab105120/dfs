Background

Every year select high schools must decide which teachers would be promoted for tenure. This year, in an initiative to improve its tenure selection process, the tenure board at Kingston High School (K.H.S) * decided to solicit outside input on its promotion decisions.


We ask for your help!


In what follows we ask you to answer basic questions about your experience with providing performance reviews. Afterwards, you will be asked to evaluate teachers' job performance based on reviews provided by the teacher's supervisors. The first {{ practiceRounds }} evaluations are for practice. The purpose of these rounds is to familiarize yourself with the exercise. At the end of each practice round you will receive feedback on your evaluation, please read the feedback carefully. Once the practice rounds are complete you will proceed to final evaluations of actual teacher performance profiles.
